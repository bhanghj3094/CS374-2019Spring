About Promiss

Promiss is a web to help users who like SNS and really want to cut off dependency(alcohol, cigarette, or caffeine) in the temporary period.

Function1: Choose your dependency and the duration to cut-off.
Function2: Set your spending habit and goals for your motivation.
Function3: Let your friends know about your determination!
Function4: Suppose you want to stop your challenge for a while. Find a way to do it. (soft-failure)


Check out the developer's note for more information.


Promiss is in a Beta release.
