About Promiss

Promiss is a web to help users who like SNS and really want to cut off dependency(alcohol, cigarette, or caffeine)
in the temporary period.

Function1: You can set up your goal and check how much you have achieved every time.
Function2: You can share your goal at SNS, and interact with your friends.
Function3: You can play fun games to get a chance.
Function4: You can gradually have a happier life!


Check out the developer's note for more information.


Promiss is in a Beta release.
